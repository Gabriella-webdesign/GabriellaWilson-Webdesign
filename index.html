The Beauty of Fall

Created by Gabriella Wilson

Now that the fall season has started,
the sweltering hot days have departed.
The sun goes down earlier than usual.
But that makes sunsets waymore beautiful.
Outstanding colors are everywhere,
it is hard not to stop and stare.
The colors of fall: orange, yellow, white and red,
makes me want to spend my time outside instead.
The most important thing about fall,
is how happy it makes us all.
